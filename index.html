About The Consultancy

Experienced Engineering Knowledge with Modern Digital Capability

Kabir Engineering & Technical Consultancy is established to provide reliable senior-level engineering, technical documentation and digital support to companies, contractors, consultants, investors and business organizations.

Founded by Engr. A.T. Kabir Uddin Ahmed, KETC combines practical power and industrial project experience with a multidisciplinary network of specialist associates covering mechanical, electrical, civil, infrastructure and digital technology disciplines.

KETC also works with Australian-based digital and data specialists to support AI-enhanced documentation, data science collaboration, website development and professional technical communication.
