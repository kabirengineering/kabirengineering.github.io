About KETC

Engineering experience supported by modern digital capability

Kabir Engineering & Technical Consultancy (KETC) provides senior-level engineering, project documentation and digital support for companies, contractors, consultants, investors and business organizations.

Founded by Engr. A.T. Kabir Uddin Ahmed, KETC combines practical power, industrial and construction project experience with a multidisciplinary network of specialist associates covering engineering, documentation, data and digital technology disciplines.

The public positioning is professional and client-focused: KETC delivers integrated consultancy through experienced professionals and specialist associates, without presenting private family details on the website.

Consultancy Workflow

How KETC delivers professional support

Every assignment follows a practical quality-focused workflow so clients receive clear, reviewed and usable deliverables.

01

Requirement Review

Understand project objectives, existing documents, timeline, stakeholders and expected output.

02

Technical Structuring

Define scope, content hierarchy, engineering assumptions, report format and required attachments.

03

Specialist Input

Coordinate engineering, digital or data-related inputs from relevant professionals and specialist associates.

04

AI-Assisted Preparation

Use modern digital and AI tools where helpful to improve clarity, speed, structure and consistency.

05

Professional Review

Review wording, technical logic, presentation, formatting and client suitability before delivery.

06

Client-Ready Delivery

Submit polished documents, website content, reports or advisory notes ready for professional use.
