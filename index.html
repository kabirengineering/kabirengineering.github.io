About The Consultancy

Experienced Engineering Knowledge with Modern Documentation Support

Kabir Engineering & Technical Consultancy is established to provide reliable senior-level engineering and technical documentation support to companies, contractors, consultants, investors and business organizations.

Founded by Engr. A.T. Kabir Uddin Ahmed, KETC combines practical power project experience with modern digital documentation tools including Microsoft Office, Canva, Adobe PDF and AI-assisted technical writing support.
